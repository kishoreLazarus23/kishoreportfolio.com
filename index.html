About Me

Experience 4 Years 2D Animator & 3D Artist
Education B.Com Bachelor degree 3 Years BFA Animation Fine & Arts 4 Years

I am a passionate 2D & 3D Animation Artist and Visual Storyteller with a strong focus on creating meaningful, cinematic, and emotionally driven visuals. My work spans 2D animation, character design, motion graphics, and VFX, with special interest in drama, inspirational themes, political narratives, and action-based storytelling. I believe in traditional creative processes—from concept development and rough sketches to final animation and visual effects—without relying on AI generators. This allows me to maintain originality, control, and artistic integrity in every project. Through my animations, I aim to communicate powerful stories, social messages, and human emotions in a visually engaging way. I continuously explore new techniques and styles to improve my craft and deliver high-quality, professional results suitable for films, digital platforms, and creative media.
